Delete Your Outfit AI Account

You can permanently delete your Outfit AI account and associated data at any time.

1

Delete Your Account Inside the App

You can delete your Outfit AI account directly from within the app in a few steps:

  1. Open the Outfit AI app on your Android device.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
  5. Wait until the deletion process completes. You will be signed out automatically once it's done.
2

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account (linked to Google Sign-In)
  • Your Firestore profile
  • Remaining credits associated with your account
  • Your AI generation history
  • Saved preferences and settings
  • Locally cached data stored on your device
  • Uploaded images stored by the app (if applicable)
  • Any other cloud data associated with your account
3

What Data May Be Retained

A small amount of information may be retained after account deletion, for reasons outside our direct control or as required by law:

  • Google Play purchase history cannot be deleted by us, as it is managed directly by Google.
  • RevenueCat may retain subscription transaction identifiers required for subscription validation and fraud prevention.
  • Some information may be retained temporarily where required by applicable law or regulation.
4

Subscription Information

Deleting your Outfit AI account does not cancel your Google Play subscription. Your subscription is managed separately by Google Play and will continue to renew until you cancel it directly.

Important: Please cancel your subscription before or after deleting your account to avoid future charges.

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app.
  2. Go to Payments & subscriptions.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Select Outfit AI.
  5. Tap Cancel subscription.
